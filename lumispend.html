Project Overview

Lumispend is an intelligent expense tracking application that eliminates the friction of manual data entry through AI-powered voice recognition and receipt scanning, making financial management truly effortless.

What is Lumispend?

Lumispend is a personal finance app that tracks expenses and income with unprecedented ease. Using AI technology, users can add transactions via voice commands or by simply scanning receipts, removing the tedious manual entry that makes people abandon expense trackers.

The Opportunity

Most expense tracker apps fail because they require too much effort from users. Every transaction means stopping what you're doing to manually type details. This friction causes abandonment. Lumispend solves this with AI-powered input methods that take seconds, not minutes.

My Role

As the Product Designer and Developer, I handled the complete product lifecycle from user research and interface design to building the mobile app with React Native and integrating AI features for voice recognition and OCR receipt scanning.

The Challenge

Why do people abandon expense tracker apps?

  • Manual data entry feels like work - too time-consuming after every purchase
  • Breaking momentum - pulling out phone to type kills the convenience of mobile payments
  • Forgotten transactions - by the time users get home, they've forgotten half their spending
  • Category fatigue - deciding which category for each expense creates decision paralysis
  • No immediate value - effort required upfront, insights come much later

The Solution

AI-powered input that makes tracking effortless:

  • Voice Entry - Speak your transaction naturally, AI extracts all details automatically
  • Receipt Scanning - Snap a photo of receipts, OCR technology extracts and categorizes everything
  • Home Screen Widgets - Add transactions without opening the app
  • Calendar View - Visual timeline shows spending patterns at a glance
  • Smart Categorization - AI suggests categories based on merchant names and patterns
  • Budget Alerts - Real-time progress bars show spending against monthly budgets

User Flow

Seamless journey from onboarding to daily expense tracking with multiple input methods.

User Journey Highlights:

1. Splash screen → Onboarding (to be added) → Direct to home
2. View financial overview with balance card, budget progress, and recent transactions
3. Filter transactions by categories (Food, Transport, Shopping, etc.)
4. Add transaction via three methods:
   • Manual Entry: Select expense/income, amount, category, description, date/time
   • Voice Entry: Speak transaction details, AI extracts and populates form
   • Receipt Scan: Photo receipt, OCR extracts merchant, amount, date automatically
5. View transactions in detailed list or calendar view with date highlighting
6. Edit transactions by swiping left on any item
7. View statistics with spending breakdown by category
8. Manage settings (budget, currency, dark/light mode, data backup)

Key Features

Innovative features designed to eliminate friction and make expense tracking feel effortless.

AI Voice Entry

The game-changing feature that makes Lumispend different. Simply speak your transaction naturally - "I spent 5000 naira on groceries at Shoprite today" - and AI extracts amount, category, merchant, and date automatically. The system processes your voice, validates the data, then presents a pre-filled form for quick confirmation. No typing required.

Smart Receipt Scanning

Turn paper receipts into digital records instantly. Point your camera at any receipt and OCR technology extracts merchant name, purchase amount, date, and even individual items. Perfect for post-shopping logging without manual typing. The AI automatically categorizes based on merchant recognition.

Dual Transaction Views

View your spending history two ways. Detailed List shows every transaction with category icons, amounts (color-coded red for expenses, green for income), and timestamps. Calendar View displays a monthly calendar with date highlighting - pink for expenses, blue for income, yellow for mixed days. Tap any date to see that day's transactions below.

Category Filtering & Quick Stats

Home screen displays category chips (Food, Transport, Shopping, etc.) at the top. Tap any category to instantly filter all transactions showing only that spending type. The balance card shows current balance, total expenses, and income at a glance. Budget progress bars visualize monthly spending limits with percentage indicators.

Flexible Theme Modes

Full light and dark mode support with beautiful gradient accents that work in both themes. Dark mode features the signature pink-to-purple gradient against deep backgrounds, perfect for late-night expense logging. Light mode offers a clean, professional look for daytime use. Switch instantly in settings.

Key Learnings & Reflections

The biggest insight from designing Lumispend was understanding that people don't hate tracking expenses - they hate the work it requires. Every expense tracker promises to help users save money, but most fail because they create more friction than value. The solution wasn't better charts or fancier analytics - it was making input so fast it feels invisible.

Voice entry and receipt scanning aren't just "nice features" - they're fundamental to solving the abandonment problem. When logging a transaction takes 5 seconds instead of 30, users actually do it. When the app extracts data automatically instead of forcing multiple form fields, it respects people's time. This is what makes Lumispend different from the dozens of abandoned expense trackers on users' phones.

Building the AI integration taught me the importance of graceful error handling. Voice recognition isn't perfect, and receipt photos can be blurry. The key was designing the confirmation step - letting AI do the heavy lifting but always giving users a chance to review and correct before saving. This balance between automation and control is crucial for trust.

Looking forward, I want to explore predictive features - using transaction patterns to automatically suggest budgets, warn about unusual spending, and forecast month-end balances. The calendar view could show future dates with projected expenses based on recurring patterns. The home screen widget could also evolve into an iOS/Android widget for truly instant logging without opening the app.
