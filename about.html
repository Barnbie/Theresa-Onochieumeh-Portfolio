About Me

Designer, Developer, and Problem Solver

Hello, I'm Theresa!

I’m a web developer with a background in product design. My journey into tech did not follow a straight line, and I think that’s one of my strengths. I started building websites with WordPress years ago, working on small projects and learning by doing. Later, I moved into product design professionally, where I learned how to think deeply about usability, structure, and user behavior. Over time, I felt the need to go beyond the surface and understand the logic behind the interface. That curiosity pushed me deeper into development.

Today, I focus primarily on web development. I build websites and web applications using WordPress and programming languages, and I am currently expanding into mobile app development. Because of my design background, I do not just think about whether something works. I think about whether it makes sense, whether it feels clear, and whether it truly serves the person using it.

I care a lot about structure and clarity. I like knowing how things are put together. I like solving problems that require both logic and empathy. That balance between technical depth and human understanding is what shapes the way I work.

Over the years, I have worked with startups, small businesses, and growing teams, helping them strengthen their digital presence through both development and design. Whether I am writing code or refining a layout, I approach each project with intention and a strong sense of ownership.

Outside of work, I am constantly learning. I explore new technologies, experiment with automation tools, and study how digital products evolve. I am not just interested in building things. I am interested in building things properly.

I explored themes, plugins, layout structures, and basic frontend customization, gradually understanding not just how to design a site visually but how to make it functional. This was where I first discovered how much I enjoyed bringing ideas to life on the web.

2022 – 2024
