Project Overview

AppFocus Cooperative Multi-Purpose Society needed a modern, trustworthy digital presence to serve their 23,000+ members across Nigeria. As a financial cooperative offering loans, savings, investments, and payments, they required a website that would build credibility, educate members about their services, and provide easy access to their mobile app and USSD banking.

The Challenge

AppFocus needed to establish a strong digital presence that would:

  • Build trust as a legitimate financial cooperative
  • Clearly explain cooperative membership benefits
  • Showcase multiple financial products (loans, savings, investments)
  • Highlight unique features like USSD banking (*7006*19#)
  • Drive mobile app downloads
  • Present social proof through member testimonials

The Solution

I developed a custom WordPress website featuring:

  • Modern, trust-building design with blue/purple brand colors
  • Dedicated product pages for each service
  • Member testimonials carousel
  • Clear 3-step onboarding flow
  • USSD banking prominently featured
  • Mobile app download CTAs
  • Comprehensive FAQ and help center

Design Process

A methodical approach to creating a trustworthy financial platform

Design Process

1. Research & Understanding: Studied the cooperative society model and identified key differentiators from traditional banks. Analyzed member needs and pain points in accessing financial services.

2. Content Strategy: Organized information architecture around Products, Company, and Resources. Emphasized trust signals like member count, funds disbursed, and testimonials.

3. Visual Design: Created a professional design system using blue/purple gradients to convey trust and innovation. Designed custom icons and graphics for each financial product.

4. Development: Built on WordPress with custom Elementor components. Implemented smooth animations, testimonial carousels, and responsive mobile design.

5. Optimization: Ensured fast loading times, mobile responsiveness, and clear calls-to-action for app downloads and member registration.

Results & Impact

Key Outcomes

The website successfully positioned AppFocus as a credible, modern financial cooperative. By establishing a professional digital presence, the platform clearly differentiated AppFocus from traditional banks while highlighting unique value propositions like USSD banking, quick loans, and high savings interest rates.

Trust was built through prominent member testimonials and transparent information about the cooperative's 23,000+ active members and ₦2.5B+ in disbursed funds. Clear pathways for app downloads and member registration, combined with a fully responsive design, ensured accessibility across all devices and improved member acquisition.
