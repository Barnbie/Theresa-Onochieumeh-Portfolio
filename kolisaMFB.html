Kolisa Microfinance Bank

A comprehensive banking website built to provide seamless financial services and build customer trust

Role
Web Developer & Designer
Duration
6 Weeks
Platform
Web (WordPress)
Tools
WordPress, Elementor, Figma

Project Overview

Kolisa Microfinance Bank needed a modern, information-rich website that would not only look visually appealing but also serve as a comprehensive resource for potential customers. The bank wanted to establish trust and credibility while providing detailed information about all their financial products and services.

The Challenge

Microfinance banks often struggle with digital presence and customer trust. The challenge was to create a website that:

  • Provides comprehensive product information without overwhelming users
  • Builds trust and credibility for a financial institution
  • Offers an engaging, modern user experience
  • Clearly showcases multiple banking products and services
  • Converts visitors into account holders

The Solution

I developed a fully custom WordPress website using Elementor and Astra theme, creating:

  • Clean, modern design with custom graphics and animations
  • Detailed product pages for each banking service
  • Interactive account opening flow visualization
  • Customer testimonials carousel
  • Comprehensive FAQ section
  • Mobile-responsive design across all pages

Design Process

A methodical approach to creating a trustworthy banking platform

Design Process

1. Research & Planning: Analyzed competitor banking websites and identified key information customers need before opening an account. Mapped out the entire site structure and content requirements.

2. Visual Design: Created custom graphics, icons, and images aligned with the brand's red color scheme. Ensured every visual element reinforced trust and professionalism.

3. Development: Built the site using WordPress with Elementor page builder, implementing custom CSS for unique interactions and animations. Integrated Lottie Files for smooth animations in the hero section.

4. Content Organization: Structured information to be easily digestible while remaining comprehensive. Created clear navigation with dropdown menus for easy access to all products and services.

5. Testing & Launch: Thoroughly tested across devices and browsers, optimized for performance, and ensured all interactive elements functioned smoothly.

Tech Stack & Tools

WordPress Elementor Astra Theme Custom CSS JavaScript Lottie Files Figma (Icons & Image Design) Canva

Key Features

📱 Comprehensive Product Pages

Created dedicated pages for Personal Accounts, Payments, Debit Cards, Savings, Loans, and USSD banking - each with detailed information and custom graphics

🎨 Custom Visual Design

All images and graphics were custom-created or heavily customized to match the bank's red brand color and maintain visual consistency

✨ Interactive Elements

Integrated Lottie animations and an interactive "Open Account in 5 Minutes" flow with draggable progress bar

💬 Customer Testimonials

Implemented a smooth carousel showcasing real customer reviews to build trust and credibility

📋 Detailed Information Architecture

Organized content into clear sections: Products, Company (About, Contact, Careers), and Resources (Help Center, Privacy Policy)

📞 Help & Support

Created comprehensive Help Center and Contact pages with multiple communication channels for customer support

Results & Impact

Key Outcomes

The website successfully achieved its goals of providing comprehensive information while maintaining an engaging, modern user experience. Key outcomes include establishing a strong digital presence for the microfinance bank, creating trust through detailed and transparent product information, and providing clear pathways for customers to understand and access banking services.

The fully responsive, mobile-optimized experience ensures accessibility across all devices, while the scalable WordPress platform allows for easy content updates. The website effectively positions Kolisa as a professional, customer-focused financial institution ready to serve its community.
