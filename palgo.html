Project Overview

PalGo is a dual-sided marketplace mobile application that bridges the gap between users who need everyday tasks completed and service providers (Pals) who can fulfill those needs efficiently and reliably.

What is PalGo?

PalGo is an on-demand errand service platform that connects users with verified local service providers for everyday tasks ranging from grocery shopping and fuel purchases to house cleaning, car washing, plumbing, and hair styling.

The Opportunity

In today's fast-paced world, people struggle to manage daily errands alongside their busy schedules. Meanwhile, skilled individuals seeking flexible income opportunities lack a reliable platform to showcase their services.

My Role

As the Product Designer, I was responsible for the complete user experience design, from initial user research and wireframing to creating high-fidelity prototypes for both user and service provider interfaces.

The Challenge

Urban dwellers and busy professionals face significant challenges managing daily tasks and errands:

  • Limited time to complete essential errands like grocery shopping, fuel purchases, and home maintenance
  • Difficulty finding trustworthy service providers for household tasks
  • Lack of transparency in pricing and service quality
  • Safety concerns when inviting strangers to their homes
  • No centralized platform for diverse service needs

The Solution

PalGo addresses these challenges through a comprehensive two-sided marketplace:

  • Verified service providers (Pals) with background checks and ID verification
  • Real-time tracking and transparent communication throughout the errand process
  • Flexible service categories from simple errands to skilled artisan work
  • Built-in payment system with secure transactions
  • Rating and review system to ensure quality and accountability
  • Map-based interface showing available Pals nearby

User Flows

PalGo operates as a dual-sided platform with distinct user journeys for Errand Users and Service Providers (Pals).

User Journey Highlights:

1. Sign up with email verification
2. View map with available Pals
3. Request an errand by filling out a simple form
4. Review Pal profiles with ratings, verification badges, and estimated arrival times
5. Accept a Pal and track their location in real-time
6. Communicate via in-app chat and calling
7. Complete payment through secure bank transfer
8. Rate and review the service

Pal Journey Highlights:

1. Enhanced sign-up with ID verification (NIN, passport, or driver's license)
2. Complete profile with occupation, experience, and service categories
3. View available errands on map-based interface
4. Accept or decline errand requests
5. Navigate to user location with tracking
6. Complete errands and receive payments directly to wallet
7. Withdraw earnings to linked bank account
8. Build reputation through ratings and reviews

Key Features

Thoughtfully designed features that prioritize safety, transparency, and seamless user experience.

Map-Based Pal Discovery

Users can visualize available Pals in their area through an interactive map interface. Icons represent different service categories, making it easy to find the right help at the right time. Real-time location updates ensure accurate proximity information.

Smart Errand Request Form

A streamlined form allows users to specify errand details including task description, location, timing preferences (immediate or scheduled), estimated duration, and budget range. The interface adapts based on the type of service requested.

Comprehensive Pal Profiles

Detailed profiles display verification badges (ID, phone, background check), star ratings, skills and capabilities, acceptance and completion rates, reviews from previous users, and estimated arrival time. This transparency builds trust and helps users make informed decisions.

Real-Time Tracking & Communication

Once a Pal is accepted, users can track their location in real-time, similar to ride-sharing apps. In-app chat and calling features enable seamless communication. Safety features include emergency contact options and the ability to share trip details.

Integrated Wallet System (Pals)

Service providers have access to a dedicated earnings dashboard showing wallet balance, transaction history, and withdrawal options. They can link bank accounts for direct deposits, view earnings analytics, and manage their financial information securely.

Key Learnings & Reflections

Designing PalGo presented unique challenges in creating a dual-sided marketplace that serves two distinct user groups with different goals and pain points. The most critical aspect was building trust through verification systems, transparent profiles, and real-time tracking features.

One of my proudest achievements was designing an intuitive errand request flow that simplified what could have been a complex process. By replacing the initial search bar concept with a direct "Request an Errand" button, we eliminated unnecessary friction and made the interface more action-oriented.

The project reinforced the importance of safety features in on-demand service platforms. Elements like background checks, verification badges, real-time tracking, and emergency contacts aren't just features—they're fundamental to user confidence and adoption.

Looking forward, I would love to explore features like AI-powered Pal matching based on user preferences and past experiences, integration with smart home devices for automated errand requests, and a subscription model for frequent users.
